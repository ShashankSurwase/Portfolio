About Me

I'm a Data Engineer & Analytics Engineer who has spent the last five years — inside a data-analytics consultancy — building data systems for six client companiesacross e-commerce, edtech, and renewable energy. In each one the story was the same: the business was scaling fast but couldn't trust its numbers — so I built the whole stack that fixed that.

I own every layer — from raw API, IoT and file ingestion, through Airflow orchestration and SQL warehouses, to the Grafana, Power BI and Superset dashboards business teams make decisions on. What I care about most is reliable, traceable numbers: schema-validated pipelines, match-rate monitoring and standards-audited KPIs, so every figure on the dashboard holds up.

Along the way I've recovered $1.05M in lost revenue, cut new-source onboarding from days to hours, and taken 120+ hours of manual work off the table every month — the kind of impact that comes from owning every problem end to end.
